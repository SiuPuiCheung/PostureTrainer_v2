## Key Gaps to Address for Your MVP
- Scalable architecture (multi-user, cloud/API support)
- Modern, accessible UI (web/mobile)
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- LLM-powered feedback (text/voice)
- Extensible model integration (YOLOv11, future models)
- User management and privacy controls
